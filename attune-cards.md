# Attune — Card Deck Content
**200 cards across 6 categories · v1.0**

## 🌱 Warmup
*30 cards · Type: Questions · Level 1*

Easy, playful, low-stakes questions to ease into the evening. Think of these as "how was your day" — but actually interesting.

1. What's your comfort meal when life feels hard?
2. What song takes you back to a specific memory instantly?
3. What's something you're quietly proud of that you rarely talk about?
4. Describe your perfect lazy Sunday.
5. What's a habit you've tried to quit but secretly don't want to?
6. What's the best gift anyone's ever given you?
7. What's your most irrational fear?
8. What's a skill you wish you had?
9. What did you want to be when you were 10?
10. What's a movie or show that shaped how you see the world?
11. What's your go-to way to decompress after a stressful day?
12. What's a small pleasure you look forward to every week?
13. What's something you find beautiful that most people overlook?
14. What's a childhood memory that still makes you smile?
15. What's your relationship with mornings like?
16. If you could live anywhere for a year, where?
17. What's a book, podcast, or idea you've been obsessed with lately?
18. What's something you do differently than most people?
19. What would a perfect birthday look like for you?
20. What's a place you visited that surprised you?
21. What's something you'd try if failure wasn't possible?
22. What's a personality trait you got from your family?
23. What's a compliment you remember from years ago?
24. What's your fantasy for retirement or a "slow life"?
25. What's something that never fails to make you laugh?
26. What's your relationship with money growing up?
27. Who in your life has had the most positive influence on you?
28. What's a small daily ritual that matters a lot to you?
29. What does "home" feel like to you?
30. What's a dream you've had more than once?

## 💬 Connection
*40 cards · Type: Questions · Level 2*

A little deeper. Progressive self-disclosure questions — the ones that make you feel like you really know someone.

1. What's something you've changed your mind about in the last 5 years?
2. What's a value you hold that you feel people don't understand about you?
3. When do you feel most like yourself?
4. What's something you need that's hard to ask for?
5. What does emotional safety feel like to you?
6. What's a goal you've been afraid to say out loud?
7. What's something from your childhood you're still working through?
8. How do you know when you're loved?
9. What's something you've always wanted to tell me but haven't?
10. When do you feel most disconnected — from me or from yourself?
11. What's a fear about our future you rarely voice?
12. What does "being known" feel like vs. "being understood"?
13. How did your parents' relationship shape what you want from ours?
14. What's something you're grieving, even quietly?
15. What's a version of yourself you've had to let go of?
16. What do you need from me when you're struggling that I sometimes miss?
17. What's the bravest thing you've ever done?
18. What makes you feel truly seen?
19. What's a disappointment you've never fully processed?
20. What's something you wish people asked you more?
21. When was the last time you cried, and why?
22. What's a belief you hold about relationships that you didn't choose — it just became part of you?
23. What's something about yourself you're still learning to accept?
24. Is there something in our relationship you've made peace with that I don't know about?
25. What's an unfulfilled dream you still carry?
26. What does vulnerability cost you? Is it worth it?
27. What's a part of yourself you protect the most?
28. How has the way you love changed since we met?
29. What's something I do that makes you feel most loved?
30. What's a moment in our relationship you return to often?
31. What would your younger self think of who you are now?
32. What's an assumption about you that people always get wrong?
33. What's something you've outgrown that still feels like a loss?
34. What matters more to you now than it did 5 years ago?
35. What's something you've never told anyone, that you could tell me?
36. What's your relationship with asking for help?
37. What does forgiveness feel like in your body?
38. What's a moment you felt completely proud of yourself?
39. What's something you wish our relationship gave you more of?
40. What's the most important lesson love has taught you?

## 🌊 Depth
*40 cards · Type: Questions · Level 3*

The real stuff. Past, values, futures, and the things left unsaid. Best with time, no phones, and something to drink.

1. What's your core fear in relationships?
2. What does commitment mean to you — has that changed?
3. What's something unresolved between us you wish we'd address?
4. How do you handle feeling misunderstood by me?
5. What's your first instinct when you feel criticised?
6. What's a conflict we've had that you think we didn't fully repair?
7. What are your non-negotiables — the things that, if lost, would break you?
8. How did your family handle conflict? How do you see that showing up in us?
9. What's your relationship with anger?
10. What does "enough" mean to you — in work, love, life?
11. Is there something you want that you're afraid to want?
12. What's a dream for our relationship you haven't named yet?
13. What's a sacrifice you've made for this relationship that I might not fully see?
14. What would you want me to fight for if things got hard?
15. What does security feel like to you — and do you feel it with me?
16. What's a chapter of your life you'd like me to understand better?
17. What's something that happened to you that changed your relationship to trust?
18. Where do you want us to be in 10 years — not just life logistics, but emotionally?
19. What do you think I need that I don't ask for?
20. What's a way I've grown that you've noticed but maybe haven't said?
21. What parts of your identity feel unseen in our relationship?
22. Is there something you've been pretending is okay that isn't?
23. What's a resentment, however small, you've been holding?
24. What's the most loving thing I've ever done for you?
25. What's your relationship with loneliness — even within a relationship?
26. When did you first feel like you really loved me?
27. What's an argument we've had that was actually about something else?
28. What does "home" mean in the context of us — people, not places?
29. What's the thing you most want me to understand about you?
30. Is there a version of the future you've let go of that you mourn?
31. What would you want me to say to you on your worst day?
32. What's something you want more courage to say to me?
33. What's a way I've hurt you that I may not know about?
34. What's something you admire about how I love you?
35. What's a way your love for me surprised you?
36. What does it mean to truly respect someone — and do you feel respected by me?
37. What do you need when you're pulling away?
38. What does a "good fight" look like to you?
39. What's a fear about yourself in relationships?
40. What's your love story as you'd tell it — what's the version in your head?

## 🔥 Physical & Intimacy
*30 cards · Type: Questions · Level 4*

Honest conversations about closeness and what you need. These aren't dares — they're questions that open doors.

1. When do you feel most physically connected to me?
2. What kind of touch feels most loving vs. most arousing — are they the same?
3. Is there something you've wanted to ask for physically but haven't?
4. What makes you feel safe enough to be fully present during intimacy?
5. What's something that used to feel good that doesn't anymore, or vice versa?
6. When do you feel most physically insecure, and what helps?
7. Is there a part of your body you wish I paid more attention to?
8. What does desire feel like for you — how does it build?
9. What's something you find deeply attractive about me that's not physical?
10. Is there something in our physical life you'd like to explore together?
11. What's something that makes you instantly feel less in the mood — that I could be aware of?
12. Do you feel like we're physically in sync right now? What's one thing that would bring us closer?
13. What's your relationship with initiating — is it easy or hard, and why?
14. What role does non-sexual physical touch play in how connected you feel?
15. What's something you'd want me to do more, less, or differently?
16. Is there a fantasy or scenario you've been curious about?
17. What's something about your own sexuality you're still learning?
18. Do you feel like you can tell me what you want in the moment? What would make that easier?
19. What's the most intimate moment we've had that wasn't sexual?
20. What's something physical or sensory that instantly puts you at ease?
21. Is there something in your history that's shaped how you show up in intimacy now?
22. What does feeling desired feel like for you?
23. What would you want more of in our physical relationship?
24. Is there something I do that makes you feel especially confident or beautiful?
25. What's your relationship with your own body right now?
26. When do you feel most uninhibited with me?
27. Is there a way we could slow down and be more present together physically?
28. What's something you wish I knew about what you need after sex?
29. What's a small physical ritual that feels really meaningful to you?
30. What makes you feel genuinely wanted — not just needed?

## 🎯 Challenges
*30 cards · Type: Activities · Level 5*

Things you actually do together. Put the cards down and be present with each other.

1. Give your partner a 2-minute shoulder massage, no talking.
2. Tell your partner three specific things you find beautiful about them right now.
3. Hold eye contact for 2 minutes without looking away or laughing.
4. Take turns giving each other one genuine compliment you've been holding back.
5. Write down one thing you appreciate about your partner — share without explaining.
6. Put on a song that reminds you of your partner. Let them hear it.
7. Plan a 15-minute date for this week — no phones, no agenda.
8. Tell your partner about a moment they don't know they helped you through.
9. Give each other a hand massage for 5 minutes each.
10. Say "I love you" in 3 different ways — words, a gesture, and a physical act.
11. Tell your partner one way they've made you a better person.
12. Create a 3-song playlist that describes your relationship. Share and explain.
13. Sit back to back, lean into each other, and breathe together for 2 minutes.
14. Each describe your first date from your own perspective.
15. Write a 3-line poem about your partner. Read it aloud.
16. Tell your partner what you'd post about them if you were completely unfiltered.
17. Take a photo together — something that captures how you feel right now.
18. Tell your partner your favourite physical feature about them — plus why it goes deeper.
19. Ask your partner: "What would make you feel loved this week?"
20. Cook something together with no recipe. See what happens.
21. Go for a 20-minute walk and ban complaining — only what's good.
22. Each share a rose, thorn, and bud from your week.
23. Do 5 minutes of synchronised breathing.
24. Take turns giving feedback: one thing I love about how you communicate, one thing I'd love more of.
25. Name one ritual you want to create together.
26. Pick a place you've never been — plan a trip to it, even if it's just a dream.
27. Ask each other: "What's something we used to do that you miss?"
28. Exchange phones, pick a song from each other's library, dance to it.
29. Give each other a genuine apology for something small.
30. Spend 5 minutes just holding each other — no goal, no talking.

## 💑 Spicy
*30 cards · Type: Dares · Level 6*

Romantic dares and intimate moments. Ranges from sweet to steamy — follow your mood.

1. Take turns telling each other one specific thing you find irresistible about them.
2. Give your partner a slow, deliberate kiss — no rushing.
3. Describe in detail what you find most attractive about your partner right now.
4. Spend 10 minutes exploring touch with no goal of it leading anywhere.
5. Ask your partner to show you how they like to be kissed.
6. Tell your partner about a fantasy you've never shared.
7. Take turns slowly tracing each other's hand — just notice the sensation.
8. Send your partner a flirty text from across the room.
9. Give each other a full-body, non-sexual massage for 10 minutes each.
10. Ask: "What's one thing that would make you feel wanted right now?"
11. Take turns being in charge — one partner leads for the evening, one follows.
12. Tell your partner what you were thinking about them the last time you felt desire.
13. Recreate your first kiss.
14. Blindfold your partner and introduce them to 3 different sensations — textures, temperatures, whatever feels interesting.
15. Write a note describing what you want to do together and leave it somewhere to be found.
16. Take turns saying what you want — no asking, just telling.
17. Spend 5 minutes just looking at each other in low light — no phones, no distraction.
18. Tell your partner the most attractive non-physical thing they did this week.
19. Read each other something romantic — a poem, a passage, a song.
20. Ask your partner to show you one way they want to be touched.
21. Give each other a slow neck and shoulder kiss.
22. Choose a word to use tonight as a playful "yes."
23. Tell your partner about a dream you had about them — real or invented.
24. Spend 15 minutes in a bath or shower together — no agenda.
25. Let your partner undress you slowly — take turns.
26. Tell your partner one specific moment they made you feel incredibly attractive.
27. Make out for 5 minutes like you have nowhere else to be.
28. Ask: "What would you want me to surprise you with?"
29. Create a relationship bucket list of experiences you want to share.
30. Write each other a love letter. Trade. Read aloud.
